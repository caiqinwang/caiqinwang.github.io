I’m currently a PhD student in the Rauch Lab at the University of Massachusetts Amherst, where I study Alzheimer’s disease at the molecular level. My research focuses on elucidating Tau–LRP1 interactions and developing small-molecule inhibitors to disrupt Tau uptake, a key driver of disease progression. I’m also passionate about designing and developing biochemical assays that enable mechanistic insights Tau-LRP1 interactions and therapeutic inhibitors discovery.
